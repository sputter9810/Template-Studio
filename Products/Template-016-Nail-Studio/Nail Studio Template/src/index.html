FAQ

Answer the questions that usually slow down the booking decision.

Can this template work for a solo nail tech?

Yes. The structure suits solo artists, home studios, boutique salons, and small teams with only minor copy changes.

Can I replace these placeholders with my own nail photos?

Yes. The gallery is intentionally simple so you can swap in real client work, studio images, or treatment close-ups quickly.

Does the contact form handle live bookings automatically?

No. It is a static demo form that you can connect to your preferred form handler, booking system, or backend later.
