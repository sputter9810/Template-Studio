FAQ

Answer the early questions that often block an enquiry.

Can this template be used by a solo agent or a full agency?

Yes. It works well for a solo operator, boutique office, leasing team, buyer’s agent, or broader property service brand.

Can the listings section be swapped for recent sales or suburb profiles?

Absolutely. The card structure is intentionally flexible, so it can represent active listings, sold results, service offers, or local market snapshots.

Is the contact form connected to a live backend?

No. It is a styled static form ready to connect to your preferred backend, CMS, or third-party form handler.
