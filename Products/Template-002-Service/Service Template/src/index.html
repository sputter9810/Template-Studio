What kind of businesses is this template for?

It suits local service businesses such as electricians, plumbers, builders, landscapers, painters, cleaners, and general contractors.

Can I change the colours and branding?

Yes. The CSS is split into base, layout, component, and section files so you can update brand colours, typography, and visuals quickly.

Can I remove sections I do not need?

Yes. The page uses reusable sections and components so you can delete or reorder blocks without rebuilding the whole layout.
