FAQ

Answer the common questions before they turn into friction.

Can this template work for a bakery or espresso bar?

Yes. The structure is flexible enough for cafes, bakeries, coffee carts, brunch venues, and other small hospitality businesses.

Can I replace the placeholder panels with real imagery?

Yes. You can replace the visual blocks with your own photos or convert them into image-based panels while keeping the same overall structure.

Does the contact form submit anywhere yet?

No. It is a static demo form, ready to be connected to your preferred backend, email handler, or form service.
