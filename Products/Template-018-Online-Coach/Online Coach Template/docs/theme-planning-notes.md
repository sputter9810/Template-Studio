# Theme Planning Notes

## Default direction
- Premium, calm, authority-led
- Built for online coaches, mentors, and digital product sellers
- Focus on booked calls, lead magnet capture, and trust-building proof

## Theme variants
- Plum Authority: deep plum base with coral and aqua accents for a premium flagship look
- Forest Ledger: dark forest palette with gold warmth for strategic and grounded positioning
- Navy Lift: clean navy and aqua palette for a sharper modern-business feel
- Coral Momentum: warmer contrast for bold founders and launch-focused brands
- Soft Light: brighter neutral presentation for buyers who prefer a lighter finish

## Typography direction
- Fraunces for refined high-impact headings
- Manrope for readable body copy and UI text
