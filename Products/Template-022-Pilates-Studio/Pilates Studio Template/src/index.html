FAQ

Answer the questions that often slow down a first booking.

Does this template work for both reformer and mat studios?

Yes. The class, pricing, and schedule sections are written broadly enough to support reformer-only studios, mixed-format spaces, or boutique movement brands with private sessions.

Can buyers replace the timetable with their own booking platform link?

Yes. The schedule is static by default, but it is structured so buyers can swap in embedded booking tools or external timetable links later.

Is the contact form connected to live bookings?

No. It is a static demo form designed to be connected to a preferred backend, booking platform, or form handler before launch.
