FAQ

Answer the questions that usually sit between hunger and hitting order now.

Can this template work for pizza, burgers, sushi, chicken, bowls, or other takeaway concepts?

Yes. The shared menu cards, offer section, and hours block are broad enough for most takeaway food concepts with only light copy and image changes.

Does the template include live online ordering?

No. This is a static template, so order buttons and forms are designed to be connected to a preferred ordering app, direct-order flow, inbox, or backend before launch.

Can buyers turn the offer cards into combo deals, family bundles, or lunch specials?

Yes. The pricing-style section is intentionally flexible, so it can present deals, combo packs, bundle offers, or featured menu paths without changing the page structure.
