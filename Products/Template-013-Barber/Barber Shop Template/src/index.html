FAQ

Answer the quick questions that help clients commit.

Can this template work for a solo barber?

Yes. The team section can be reduced to one card and the rest of the structure still works cleanly for an individual operator.

Can I swap the placeholders for real haircut and shop images?

Yes. The visual panels are there so buyers can quickly replace them with their own shop, service, or team photography.

Does the form take live appointments by default?

No. It is a static demo form that is ready to connect to your preferred booking tool or form handler.
