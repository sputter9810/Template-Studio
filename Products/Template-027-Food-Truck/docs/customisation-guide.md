# Customisation Guide

## Quick Start
Edit `src/index.html` to update:
- truck name and brand initials
- hero headline, supporting text, and primary CTA
- menu highlight cards and pricing
- catering offers and event-fit content
- weekly stop schedule, location notes, and testimonials
- contact details and enquiry copy

## Design Tokens
Update colours in `src/assets/css/base/variables.css`.

Update typography in `src/assets/css/base/typography.css`.

## Images
Replace the placeholder visual panels in `src/index.html` with real truck photography, close-up food shots, event scenes, market service imagery, or branding that matches the food style.

## Form
The enquiry form is static by default. Connect it to your preferred CRM, inbox, backend, or form service before going live.

## Theme Selector
To change the default theme, update the `data-theme` value on the `<body>` tag and the `defaultTheme` value in `src/assets/js/main.js`.
