FAQ

Answer the practical questions that sit between curiosity and an order.

Can this template work for both a roastery brand and a cafe that sells beans?

Yes. The featured coffee, subscription, story, and contact sections are broad enough to suit online-first coffee brands, cafe-roaster hybrids, and wholesale-led roasteries with only light copy edits.

Can buyers swap the featured cards for blends, single origins, capsules, or brew gear?

Yes. The shared card system is intentionally flexible, so this section can present coffee bags, seasonal lots, brew equipment, or curated gift sets without changing the page structure.

Does the enquiry form process live coffee orders automatically?

No. It is a static demo form designed to be connected to a preferred ecommerce flow, CRM, form handler, or backend before launch.
