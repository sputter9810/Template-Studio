FAQ

Answer the questions that usually slow down the first booking.

Is this template only suited to boxing gyms?

No. It is written to work for boxing, kickboxing, MMA, BJJ, and martial arts studios that want a stronger one-page lead generation flow.

Can I swap the placeholder visual panels with real gym photos?

Yes. The visual panels are starter placeholders so buyers can replace them with facility photos, class imagery, coach portraits, or event shots.

Do I need to keep both striking and grappling references?

No. The content is intentionally easy to swap, so a buyer can lean fully into boxing-only, kickboxing-only, MMA-focused, or BJJ-focused positioning.

Does the form handle live trial bookings by default?

No. The form is static and ready to connect to your preferred booking tool, email workflow, CRM, or form handler.
