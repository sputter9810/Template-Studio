# Solace Wellness

Solace Wellness is a customer-facing static website template for wellness businesses that want a calm, modern, trust-led online presence. It is designed as a one-page marketing site that helps visitors understand the offer, feel confident in the brand, and make an enquiry.

## Best suited for
- wellness coaches
- yoga, pilates, and mindfulness studios
- therapists and recovery providers
- massage, bodywork, and holistic service businesses
- boutique service brands in the wellness space

## What is included
- sticky header with mobile menu
- hero section with calls to action and supporting stats
- trust strip for key selling points
- services overview
- about section with trust-building content
- gallery placeholders
- step-by-step process section
- testimonials
- FAQ
- enquiry call-to-action banner
- styled contact form
- footer with quick links and contact details
