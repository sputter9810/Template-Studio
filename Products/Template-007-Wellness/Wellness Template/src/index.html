FAQ

Answer common questions before they slow down an enquiry.

Who is this template best suited to?

It is ideal for wellness coaches, studio owners, therapists, recovery businesses, and other service-based wellness brands.

Can I remove sections I do not need?

Yes. The layout is intentionally modular, so sections like gallery or FAQ can be removed without affecting the rest of the page.

Does the contact form work out of the box?

The form is styled and ready for connection, but you will need to link it to Netlify Forms, Formspree, or your own backend.
