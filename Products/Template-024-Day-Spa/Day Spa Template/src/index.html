FAQ

Answer the questions that often sit between interest and a reservation.

Can this template work for both boutique day spas and retreat-style wellness venues?

Yes. The treatment, package, testimonial, and contact sections are broad enough to suit smaller day spas, premium beauty-wellness venues, and calming retreat-style businesses with only light copy changes.

Can buyers swap the package cards for their own rituals or gift vouchers?

Yes. The package section is intentionally modular so buyers can feature massage journeys, facial packages, couple treatments, memberships, or gift voucher options without changing the layout.

Does the reservation form handle live bookings automatically?

No. It is a static demo form designed to be connected to a preferred booking system, form handler, or backend before launch.
