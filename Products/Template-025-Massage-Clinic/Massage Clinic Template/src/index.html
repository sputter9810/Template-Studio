FAQ

Answer the questions that often sit between interest and a booking.

Can this template work for both remedial clinics and more general massage practices?

Yes. The treatment, pricing, practitioner, and contact sections are broad enough to suit remedial massage clinics, sports recovery practices, relaxation-focused studios, and small wellness treatment brands.

Can buyers swap the pricing cards for their own session lengths or specialties?

Yes. The pricing section is intentionally modular so buyers can feature initial consults, shorter maintenance sessions, pregnancy massage, lymphatic support, or other treatment types without changing the layout.

Does the booking form handle live appointments automatically?

No. It is a static demo form designed to be connected to a preferred booking system, form handler, or backend before launch.
