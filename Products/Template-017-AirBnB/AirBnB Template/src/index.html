FAQ

Answer the quick questions that often block a booking enquiry.

Can this template work for one property or a small collection of stays?

Yes. It is ideal for a single hero property, but the cards and gallery can also be adapted for a boutique group of cabins, villas, or apartments.

Is there a live booking engine included?

No. This is a static front-end template designed for direct enquiries. You can connect the form to your preferred backend or link the CTAs to an external booking platform.

Can the amenities section be turned into rates or room types?

Absolutely. The card layout is intentionally flexible, so it can present amenities, rate tiers, room layouts, add-ons, or seasonal packages.
