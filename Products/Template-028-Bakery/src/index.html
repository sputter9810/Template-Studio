FAQ

Answer the practical questions that usually sit between craving the bake and placing the enquiry.

Can this template work for artisan bread shops, pastry cafes, or a bakery with coffee service?

Yes. The structure is intentionally broad enough to support bread-led bakeries, pastry counters, celebration-cake businesses, and bakery-cafe hybrids with only light content changes.

Does the template include live online ordering?

No. This is a static brochure-style template, so the order form is designed to be connected to a preferred form handler, inbox, CRM, or ordering workflow before launch.

Can buyers replace the offer cards with cake tiers, pastry boxes, or catering packages?

Yes. The shared card system is flexible enough to present featured bakes, order bundles, wholesale options, or catering tiers without changing the underlying layout.
