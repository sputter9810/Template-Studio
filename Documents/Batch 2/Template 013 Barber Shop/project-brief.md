# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Barber Shop Template

## Niche / Industry
Grooming — Barber Shop

## Target Customer / Buyer
Barbershop owners and independent barbers

## Site Type
One-page lead generation

## Goal of the Website
Drive appointment bookings and walk-in trust

## Pages Required
- Home

## Required Sections
- Hero
- Services
- Pricing
- Team
- Testimonials
- CTA
- Contact

## Optional Sections
- Gallery
- FAQ
- Products

## Visual Direction
Bold, masculine, polished, premium

## Content Direction
Confident, local, straightforward

## Functional Requirements
- Sticky nav with anchor links
- Appointment booking/contact form
- Service/pricing cards

## Technical Constraints
- Static HTML/CSS/JS only
- No backend required
- Fast-loading images and simple interactions

## Delivery Format
Downloadable static template pack with organised source files and buyer-facing documentation.

## Notes / Special Instructions
This template should feel immediately relevant to its niche while still following the shared production system and reusable section structure.

## Conversion Strategy
Service interest → booking

## Primary User Action
Book an appointment

## Secondary Actions
- View services
- See pricing
- Meet the team

## Trust Mechanisms
- Photo-ready venue vibe
- Client testimonials
- Service list and pricing clarity

## Content Priority Order
1. Booking CTA
2. Services and pricing
3. Brand vibe
