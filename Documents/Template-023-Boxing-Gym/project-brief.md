# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Boxing & Martial Arts Gym Template

## Niche / Industry
Boxing/Martial Arts Gym

## Target Customer / Buyer
Boxing gyms, MMA academies, BJJ schools, and martial arts studios

## Site Type
One-page lead-generation site

## Goal of the Website
Turn visitors into trial members by showing training options, coaches, atmosphere, and schedule clearly.

## Pages Required
- Home
- Optional classes/timetable
- Contact

## Required Sections
- Navigation
- Hero
- Programs
- Schedule
- Coaches
- Testimonials
- CTA
- Contact
- Footer

## Optional Sections
- Pricing
- Gallery
- FAQ
- Amenities

## Visual Direction
Bold, gritty, energetic, cinematic, disciplined, and modern without becoming cluttered.

## Content Direction
High-energy but not macho. Emphasise structure, coaching, beginner-friendliness, and confidence-building.

## Functional Requirements
- Trial form or CTA
- Schedule block
- Program cards for disciplines
- Mobile-friendly contact details

## Technical Constraints
- No heavy JS
- Static media placeholders
- Keep classes/discipline names easy to swap

## Delivery Format
Static template pack.

## Notes / Special Instructions
Should work equally well for boxing, kickboxing, MMA, or BJJ with content swaps.

## Conversion Strategy
Trial class enquiries and membership leads.

## Primary User Action
Book a free trial

## Secondary Actions
- View schedule
- Meet the coaches
- See discipline options

## Trust Mechanisms
- Coach credentials
- Class level guidance
- Student testimonials
- Visible schedule

## Content Priority Order
1. Hero + programs
2. Schedule + coaches
3. Testimonials + CTA
