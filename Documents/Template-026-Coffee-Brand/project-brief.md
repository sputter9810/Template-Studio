# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Coffee Roaster Template

## Niche / Industry
Coffee Roaster

## Target Customer / Buyer
Independent coffee roasters, specialty coffee brands, and café-roaster hybrids

## Site Type
One-page brand/product site

## Goal of the Website
Drive coffee sales, subscriptions, wholesale enquiries, or café visits with a brand-led product story.

## Pages Required
- Home
- Featured coffees
- Wholesale/contact

## Required Sections
- Navigation
- Hero
- Featured coffees
- About / roasting philosophy
- Testimonials or trust
- CTA
- Contact
- Footer

## Optional Sections
- Signature offers
- Process
- FAQ
- Hours & location
- Gallery

## Visual Direction
Rich, tactile, crafted, editorial, and product-forward with premium but grounded warmth.

## Content Direction
Confident specialty coffee copy with tasting notes, sourcing language, and approachable education.

## Functional Requirements
- Featured product cards
- Subscription/wholesale CTA
- Location details if retail exists

## Technical Constraints
- Static only
- No ecommerce engine in core
- Use buyer-friendly product placeholder copy

## Delivery Format
Static template pack for productised brand sites.

## Notes / Special Instructions
Should support direct-to-consumer sales or wholesale lead generation.

## Conversion Strategy
Buy beans, start a subscription, or enquire about wholesale.

## Primary User Action
Shop the roast

## Secondary Actions
- Explore blends
- Ask about wholesale
- Visit the café

## Trust Mechanisms
- Roasting philosophy
- Origin/sourcing cues
- Customer proof
- Signature offerings

## Content Priority Order
1. Hero + featured coffees
2. Brand story + proof
3. CTA + contact
