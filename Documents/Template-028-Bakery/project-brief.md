# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Bakery Template

## Niche / Industry
Bakery

## Target Customer / Buyer
Independent bakeries, artisan bread shops, pastry cafés, and local baked-goods businesses

## Site Type
One-page local business site

## Goal of the Website
Increase foot traffic, pre-orders, and catering or wholesale enquiries through product-led storytelling.

## Pages Required
- Home
- Signature bakes
- Contact / order enquiry

## Required Sections
- Navigation
- Hero
- Signature products
- About
- Testimonials or trust
- CTA
- Contact
- Footer

## Optional Sections
- Hours & location
- Gallery
- FAQ
- Menu highlights

## Visual Direction
Warm, handcrafted, inviting, tactile, and neighbourhood-friendly.

## Content Direction
Friendly, delicious, product-led copy with strong freshness and craft cues.

## Functional Requirements
- Featured product cards
- Hours/location block
- Order or catering CTA
- Simple contact details

## Technical Constraints
- Static only
- No ecommerce or ordering engine in core
- Keep copy flexible for daily changing menus

## Delivery Format
Static template pack.

## Notes / Special Instructions
Should support artisan bread focus, pastry/cake focus, or general bakery/café hybrid.

## Conversion Strategy
Visit the bakery, place an order, or enquire about wholesale/catering.

## Primary User Action
See what’s baking

## Secondary Actions
- Visit today
- Ask about catering
- Explore signature products

## Trust Mechanisms
- Freshness cues
- Signature product highlights
- Community/social proof
- Clear opening hours

## Content Priority Order
1. Hero + signature products
2. Brand story + hours
3. CTA + contact
