# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Massage Clinic Template

## Niche / Industry
Massage Clinic

## Target Customer / Buyer
Massage therapists, remedial clinics, sports recovery businesses, and wellness practitioners

## Site Type
One-page service website

## Goal of the Website
Drive treatment bookings and enquiries by clearly showing services, trust signals, and practical information.

## Pages Required
- Home
- Services
- Contact / booking

## Required Sections
- Navigation
- Hero
- Services
- About / qualifications
- Pricing
- Testimonials
- CTA
- Contact
- Footer

## Optional Sections
- FAQ
- Hours & location
- Process
- Team

## Visual Direction
Clean, calm, health-oriented, reassuring, and professional.

## Content Direction
Clear, practical wellness copy that balances care with credibility.

## Functional Requirements
- Service and pricing cards
- Booking CTA
- Location/hours information
- Intro note about who treatment is for

## Technical Constraints
- Static only
- No medical claims
- Accessible typography and contrast

## Delivery Format
Static clinic template pack.

## Notes / Special Instructions
Should work for remedial, relaxation, sports massage, and recovery-based services.

## Conversion Strategy
Booking enquiries and initial consult/treatment leads.

## Primary User Action
Book your appointment

## Secondary Actions
- View treatments
- See prices
- Ask a question

## Trust Mechanisms
- Practitioner profile
- Treatment benefits and use cases
- Testimonials
- Clinic details

## Content Priority Order
1. Hero + services
2. Pricing + trust
3. Contact
