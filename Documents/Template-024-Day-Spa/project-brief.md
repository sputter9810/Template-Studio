# Project Brief

## Template / Product Name
Day Spa Template

## Niche / Industry
Day Spa

## Target Customer / Buyer
Boutique day spas, premium wellness retreats, and luxury beauty/wellness venues

## Site Type
One-page premium brochure site

## Goal of the Website
Encourage treatment enquiries and package bookings with a premium, calming, trust-heavy presentation.

## Pages Required
- Home
- Treatment overview
- Contact / reservations

## Required Sections
- Navigation
- Hero
- Treatments
- Experience
- Packages or pricing
- Testimonials
- CTA
- Contact
- Footer

## Optional Sections
- Gallery
- FAQ
- Team
- Amenities
- Hours & location

## Visual Direction
Elegant, serene, premium, layered textures, soft lighting, and restrained luxury.

## Content Direction
Refined, sensory, calming copy that sells the feeling and the ritual, not just the service list.

## Functional Requirements
- Treatment cards
- Package pricing
- Reservation CTA
- Hours/location and contact details

## Technical Constraints
- Static only
- No booking engine in core
- Avoid clutter or loud contrast

## Delivery Format
Static premium template pack.

## Notes / Special Instructions
Suitable for spa rituals, facials, massage, wellness packages, and gift voucher promotion.

## Conversion Strategy
Treatment bookings and gift voucher enquiries.

## Primary User Action
Book a treatment

## Secondary Actions
- View packages
- See the spa experience
- Ask about gift vouchers

## Trust Mechanisms
- Treatment descriptions
- Atmosphere visuals
- Testimonials
- Premium service cues

## Content Priority Order
1. Hero + treatment overview
2. Packages + trust
3. Contact + reservation CTA
