About Briggs Digital

Independent website templates for real businesses.

Briggs Digital is a product-focused template brand built by Sam Briggs. The aim is simple: create modern website templates that feel polished, practical, and genuinely useful for real business websites.

The brand

Why Briggs Digital exists.

Briggs Digital was built around the idea that small businesses and niche brands should be able to launch with a website that already feels modern, structured, and professional.

Instead of generic layouts, the focus is on niche-specific templates that are designed with real business use in mind — from cafes and salons to gyms and agencies.

The builder

Built by Sam Briggs.

Briggs Digital is an independent project built product-first: clean structure, reusable sections, consistent packaging, multiple colour directions, and practical layouts that are easier to customise and launch.

The goal is not just to make templates look good, but to make them feel like real products that people can actually use.

Principles

What Briggs Digital is built around.

The brand is being shaped around a few clear ideas that guide how the templates are made, packaged, and improved over time.

Niche-first design
Clean, reusable structure
Modern visual systems

Product-style packaging

What makes the templates different

Designed as products, not just mockups.

Each template is intended to be more than a single nice-looking page. The aim is to build products with reusable sections, clear structure, multiple colour schemes, and a stronger sense of fit for the niche they are meant to serve.

That means the collection is being built with consistency in mind: naming, changelogs, documentation, packaging, and future expansion.
