# Extraction Notes

## Stable across nearly all original 10 templates
- sticky header with brand + nav + toggle
- hero with eyebrow, heading, copy, CTA pair, highlight list, stats
- trust strip
- services or menu grid
- split about section
- gallery section
- testimonials
- CTA banner
- contact split section
- footer

## Promoted in v4
- contact details block
- form note helper text
- compact feature list
- visual panel placeholder system
- listing-card variant
- about/stat grid
- visual stack pattern for photo/editorial-style heroes and media areas
- lightweight portfolio/listings treatment through section variants

## Promoted in v5
- pricing patterns for memberships, packages, and treatment/service tiers
- team/profile pattern for coach-led and trust-led businesses
- schedule and availability pattern for classes, bookings, and release cadence
- results/proof pattern for conversion-first landing pages
- amenities/inclusions pattern for rentals, venues, studios, and hospitality
- media/embed pattern for podcast and creator-style templates
- coverage/service-area pattern for local and mobile service businesses

- CrossFit and yoga needed schedules, coaches, pricing, and results

- Electrician and plumber needed pricing clarity, service areas, and proof/trust
- Airbnb / holiday rental needed amenities and locality-led sections
- Online coach needed offer, proof, and program structure sections
- Podcast website needed media-first and episode-list patterns
