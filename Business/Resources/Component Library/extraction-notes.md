# Extraction Notes

## Stable across nearly all original templates
- sticky header with brand + nav + toggle
- hero with eyebrow, heading, copy, CTA pair, highlight list, stats
- trust strip
- services or menu grid
- split about section
- gallery section
- testimonials
- CTA banner
- contact split section
- footer

## Promoted in v4 and v5
- contact details block
- form note helper text
- compact feature list
- visual panel placeholder system
- listing-card variant
- about/stat grid
- visual stack pattern
- pricing, team, schedule, results, amenities, media, and coverage patterns

## Promoted in v6
- hours-list for opening times and availability
- menu-highlight for signature items and featured offers
- reservation-note for booking and practical info
- badge-strip for quick-scan service or product flags
- hours-location-section for visit/find-us/practical info
- signature-offers-section for curation-first product and hospitality layouts

## New batch pressure points solved
- Gyms needed stronger quick-scan class, trust, and schedule cues
- Studios and clinics needed practical hours/location info
- Spas and restaurants needed featured-offer and reservation-friendly content blocks
- Roasters, bakeries, food trucks, and takeaway brands needed product-first and visit-first sections
