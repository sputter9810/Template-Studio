## General recommendation
For hospitality, wellness, and local-service templates, a strong reusable stack is:
1. hero-section
2. trust-strip or badge-strip
3. one of services / signature offers / pricing / schedule
4. one proof layer (results, testimonials, gallery)
5. one trust layer (team, about, process)
6. hours-location-section when practical info matters
7. cta-section
8. contact-section
